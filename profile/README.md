### 🏢 About Us
**Niyum Trading Co., Ltd.** is one of Cambodia's premier import, export, and distribution companies. We specialize in bringing high-quality, globally recognized FMCG products—including premium dairy like *So Natural* and *Australia's Own*, as well as *Ursu9* alkaline water—to the Cambodian market. 

Beyond our physical supply chain, we are heavily invested in building **custom digital infrastructure** to streamline modern trade (MT), HORECA relationships, and nationwide logistics.

---

### 🚀 Our Digital Infrastructure & Projects

Our in-house engineering focuses on building scalable, high-performance tools to manage complex business logic, tracking, and sales operations.

*   📦 **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) & Stock Management:** A custom-built, comprehensive platform designed to handle complex purchasing flows, landed cost tracking, multi-warehouse stock movements, sales, and consignments.
*   🗺️ **GIS Sales Tracking App:** A geographic information system leveraging React and Leaflet.js to map customer locations, plan efficient routes, and track nationwide sales visits for our distribution teams in real-time.
*   🛒 **B2B / B2C Platforms:** Dedicated product landing pages (like the upcoming Ursu9 platform) and point-of-sale (POS) integrations tailored for the Cambodian retail ecosystem.
